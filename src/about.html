MV Geek Solutions: Your local, low-cost answer to your technology problems!

MV Geek Solutions is a family-run business serving the Maple Valley, Covington, Kent and Black Diamond areas. We offer low-cost technology solutions in mobile computer repair and website design and development. It is run by husband and wife team Steve and Amanda Hayes, and we have been serving the Maple Valley area in the computer repair and web design industry for over a decade. We love our local community and have many ties to the area - we are truly proud to be a part of it.



Our Style

We take a "no-frills" approach when it comes to our business. Having a commercial office space with fancy furniture and lots of employees results in a lot of overhead that drives the price of our services too high. We want to offer our clients the lowest possible price on our technology solutions, so we work on-site or at our home office to keep costs under control and prices down. More good news: We travel to you, so you don't have to leave the convenience of your home or office. We can also meet locally to pick up or drop off your computer, in a place that is most comfortable and convenient for you.
